About This Project

Finding the right game to play can be difficult, especially in a group of friends. This website helps casual gamers discover new games based on what they’ve already played and enjoyed.

By selecting and rating games, our AI-powered system generates personalized recommendations. Game data comes from IGDB, and recommendations are powered by OpenAI.

How It Works

  • You select and rate games you've played.
  • The system fetches game details from IGDB.
  • Your selected games are sent to OpenAI, which generates personalized recommendations.

Privacy & Data Storage

This site follows GDPR regulations. No personal data is collected or stored on our servers. Your game history remains in your browser’s storage and can be deleted at any time.
