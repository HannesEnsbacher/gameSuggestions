Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 01/31/2025

1. Your Privacy Matters

We respect your privacy and comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Data Collection & Storage

We do not collect or store personal data. Your game history is saved in your browser’s local storage and is not sent to a backend server.

3. Third-Party Services

To provide recommendations, we use the following third-party services:

  • IGDB (Internet Game Database): We retrieve game data but do not send user-generated data to IGDB.
  • OpenAI: Your selected game history is sent to OpenAI to generate recommendations. No personal data is included.
  • Google Analytics: We use this service for anonymous usage data, but it requires user consent under GDPR.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Google Analytics may store cookies on your device for tracking website usage. Under GDPR, you have the right to opt in or out. We provide a cookie consent banner to manage your preferences.

5. Your Rights Under GDPR

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access any data we store about you (we do not store any).
  • Request data deletion (you can clear your game history from your browser).
  • Withdraw consent for analytics tracking.

6. Removing Your Data

You can remove your game history at any time by:

  • Clicking the "Clear Game History" button on the selection screen.
  • Manually clearing your browser’s local storage.

7. Contact

If you have any concerns about privacy, you can contact us at [your email/contact page].

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Any significant changes will be communicated on this page.
